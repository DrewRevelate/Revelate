Quality Gate Audit

56-point checklist ensuring authentically Revelate design

Red Flags: Automatic Failure

Visual Red Flags

  • Use of emojis anywhere in content
  • ANY accent left borders on cards/alerts
  • Heavy black shadows or gloss
  • Generic purple-blue gradients
  • Default blue #3B82F6

Content Red Flags

  • Buzzword overload
  • Vague value propositions
  • Generic CTAs ("Learn more")
  • No specific data/metrics
  • AI-generated filler text
