## Brand Foundation

### Our Mission

We believe that every business, regardless of size or resources, deserves the power to harness their data for strategic growth. We break down the barriers in understanding and utilizing revenue operations, providing accessible and expert-driven solutions.

### Brand Emotions

Revelate Operations evokes two primary emotions in everything we create:

#### **TRUST (70%)**
- Reliability and expertise
- Professional partnership
- Methodical precision

#### **AMAZEMENT (30%)**
- Breakthrough insights
- Revelation moments
- Transformative discovery

### Visual Strategy

**"Navy anchors, Cyan amazes"**

Our brand balances the grounding professionalism of deep navy with the energetic revelation of vibrant cyan‚Äîcreating an identity that says "We're the reliable partner who reveals incredible insights you didn't know existed."

### Brand Story

Our colors tell a story:
- **Navy:** The deep space of unexplored data‚Äîmysterious but not chaotic, rich with hidden potential
- **Cyan:** The moment the constellation emerges from scattered stars‚Äîsudden clarity, the pattern revealed
- **Magenta:** That spark of elation‚Äîthe "I can't believe we didn't see this before!" moment

> *"Like a seasoned guide leading into an undiscovered cave, where reliable torchlight suddenly illuminates ancient crystals that take your breath away."*

## Typography

### Font Families

#### Headings: Space Grotesk
**Source:** Google Font ‚Äî FREE
**Character:** Modern, geometric, tech-forward yet professional
**Weights:** Bold (700), Semibold (600), Medium (500)
**Personality:** Confident expert who commands attention

#### Body: Inter
**Source:** Google Font ‚Äî FREE
**Character:** Designed for screen readability
**Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500), Semibold (600)
**Personality:** Thorough analyst who backs up every claim

#### Monospace: JetBrains Mono (optional)
**Usage:** Code, data tables, technical specifications

### Type Scale

Base size: **16px**

| Element | Size | Font | Weight |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| H1 (Hero) | 48px / 3rem | Space Grotesk | Bold (700) |
| H2 (Section) | 36px / 2.25rem | Space Grotesk | Bold (700) |

| H4 (Card Title) | 20px / 1.25rem | Space Grotesk | Semibold (600) |

| Body Small | 14px / 0.875rem | Inter | Regular (400) |
| Caption | 12px / 0.75rem | Inter | Regular (400) |

### Typography Rules

**Line Height:**
- Headings: 1.2-1.3 (tight, impactful)
- Body text: 1.5-1.7 (readable, comfortable)
- Data tables: 1.4 (compact but clear)

**Letter Spacing:**
- Headings: -0.02em (tighter, more premium)
- Body: 0em (default)
- All caps labels: +0.05em (improves readability)

**Maximum Line Lengths:**
- Headings: 60 characters max
- Body text: 75 characters max

**Minimum Sizes:**
- Digital: Space Grotesk min 14px, Inter min 12px
- Print: Space Grotesk min 10pt, Inter min 8pt
- Business cards: 11pt minimum

### Responsive Scaling

| Breakpoint | H1 | H2 | H3 | Body |
|------------|----|----|----|----|
| Mobile (<768px) | 32px | 24px | 22px | 16px |
| Tablet (768-1024px) | 40px | 30px | 24px | 16px |
| Desktop (>1024px) | 48px | 36px | 28px | 16px |

## Logo Guidelines

### Logo Versions

1. **Primary Logo:** Full cosmic swirl + REVELATE wordmark
2. **Horizontal Lockup:** Logo mark + wordmark side-by-side
3. **Stacked Lockup:** Logo mark above wordmark
4. **Icon Only:** Cosmic swirl without text (social media, favicons)

### Clear Space

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the letter "R" on all sides.
No text, graphics, or elements within this protective zone.

### Minimum Sizes

#### Digital
- Full logo: **160px** width minimum
- Icon only: **48px √ó 48px** minimum
- Favicon: **32px √ó 32px**

#### Print
- Full logo: **1.5 inches** width minimum
- Icon only: **0.5 inches** minimum
- Business card: **0.75 inches** minimum

### Approved Backgrounds

‚úÖ Revelate Navy (primary)
‚úÖ White (secondary)
‚úÖ Light gray (#f1f5f9)
‚úÖ Dark photography with overlay

‚ùå Busy patterns
‚ùå Low contrast colors (cyan, light blue, purple)
‚ùå Gradients (except subtle navy variations)

### Logo Don'ts

Never:
- ‚ùå Rotate the logo
- ‚ùå Skew or distort proportions
- ‚ùå Change the cosmic swirl gradient colors
- ‚ùå Add effects (drop shadows, glows, outlines)
- ‚ùå Recreate or redraw the logo
- ‚ùå Place on busy backgrounds without protection
- ‚ùå Compress below minimum sizes

### File Formats

**Vector:** `.svg` (web), `.ai` / `.eps` (print)
**Raster:** `.png` (with transparency), `.jpg` (photography integration)
**Sizes:** Maintain 500px, 1000px, 2000px, 4000px width versions

## Visual Language

### Photography Style

**Subject Matter:**
- Real people in professional settings (avoid overly posed stock)
- Diverse teams collaborating around data/screens
- Close-ups of dashboards, charts, emerging insights
- Modern offices with natural light
- Hands interacting with technology

**Mood:**
- Trust: Professional, authentic, natural moments
- Amazement: Discovery reactions, focused engagement
- Avoid: Fake enthusiasm, overly polished corporate stock

**Color Treatment:**
- Prefer images with navy, blue, cyan tones
- Apply subtle cyan or blue color grading
- Avoid warm-dominant images (oranges, yellows)
- Use navy overlay at 40-60% opacity when needed

**Composition:**
- Clean, uncluttered
- Negative space for text overlay
- Rule of thirds
- Avoid centered, static compositions

### Illustration Style

**When to Use:**
- Complex RevOps concept explanations
- Process diagrams and workflows
- Service offering icons
- Infographics and data storytelling

**Style:**
- Geometric, clean lines
- Flat or subtle depth (no heavy 3D)
- Limited palette (navy, cyan, magenta, white)
- Modern, tech-forward aesthetic
- Avoid: Cartoonish, overly decorative

### Data Visualization

**Chart Color Hierarchy:**
1. Most important: Cyan (#00d9ff)
2. Comparison: Navy (#1a1f3a)
3. Secondary: Electric Blue (#0084ff)
4. Tertiary: Slate Gray (#64748b)
5. Critical highlight: Magenta (#d946ef)

**Chart Types:**
- Trend over time: Line charts (cyan primary)
- Comparison: Horizontal bar charts
- Composition: Donut charts (never pie)
- Relationship: Scatter plots (cyan dots, magenta outliers)
- Geographic: Maps with cyan heat mapping

**Design Rules:**
- White or light gray backgrounds only
- Minimal gridlines (light gray, 1px)
- Clear axis labels in Inter Regular
- Large numbers in Space Grotesk Medium
- Direct labeling preferred over legends
- Remove chart borders and unnecessary elements

### Graphic Elements

**Approved:**
- Constellation patterns (subtle connected dots)
- Data node networks (connected points)
- Subtle gradient overlays (cyan to blue, navy to darker navy)
- Geometric shapes (circles, dots, clean lines)
- Subtle grid systems

**Usage:**
- Always low opacity (10-20%) for backgrounds
- Never compete with primary content
- Maintain generous white space
- Reinforce "revelation" theme

**Prohibited:**
- ‚ùå Busy patterns or textures
- ‚ùå Rainbow gradients
- ‚ùå Decorative flourishes
- ‚ùå Clip art or generic icons
- ‚ùå Overly technical aesthetics (circuit boards, code backgrounds)
- ‚ùå **Left borders on cards, alerts, or sections** (forbidden AI pattern)

## Iconography

### Icon Style

**CRITICAL RULE: NO EMOJIS**
- ‚ùå **NEVER use emojis** (üìä, üîß, ‚ö°, etc.) in any Revelate design
- ‚úÖ **ALWAYS use custom SVG icons** that match our brand style

- Style: Line icons (2px stroke weight)
- Shape: Geometric, clean, minimal
- Size: 24px √ó 24px standard (scalable for larger uses)

- Format: SVG with proper viewBox attribute

- Lucide Icons (customizable, MIT licensed)
- Heroicons (Tailwind, MIT licensed)
- Feather Icons (minimal, MIT licensed)

### Custom Icons

**When to Create:**
- Revelate-specific service offerings
- Unique RevOps concepts
- Brand-specific visualizations

**Guidelines:**
- Match 2px line weight
- Navy and cyan only
- 24px √ó 24px artboard
- Export as SVG with proper viewBox
- Maintain optical balance

### Animation

**Principles:**
- Subtle and purposeful only
- Duration: 200-300ms
- Easing: ease-in-out
- Use for: Hover states, transitions, loading
- Avoid: Excessive motion, looping, distractions

## Writing Style

### Grammar & Mechanics

**Capitalization:**
- Sentence case for headings
- "Revenue operations" lowercase (unless starting sentence)
- "Revelate Operations" always capitalized

**Numbers:**
- Spell out one through nine
- Numerals for 10+
- Numerals for all percentages: "5% improvement"
- Commas in large numbers: "1,000"
- Use "$" symbol: "$50K"

**Abbreviations:**
- "RevOps" acceptable after first "Revenue Operations" use
- CRM, API, ROI ‚Äî no explanation needed
- Avoid excessive abbreviations

**Punctuation:**
- Oxford comma: Always
- Em dashes‚Äîno spaces
- Ellipses for trailing thought...
- Single space after periods

### Terminology

**Preferred:**
- "Revenue operations" or "RevOps"
- "Fractional" (not "part-time")
- "Data analysis" (not "analytics" usually)
- "Insights" (not "learnings" or "takeaways")

**Avoid:**
- Buzzwords: synergy, paradigm, leverage (as verb), rockstar, ninja
- Consultant jargon: deliverables, circle back, move the needle
- Vague claims: world-class, cutting-edge, revolutionary

### Content Formatting

**Lists:**
- Use for 3+ related items
- Parallel structure
- Periods if full sentences, none if fragments
- Keep items roughly equal length

**Headings:**
- Sentence case: "How we work" not "How We Work"
- Descriptive, not clever: "Pricing" not "Investment in growth"

**Links:**
- Descriptive text: "View our case studies"
- Never: "Click here," "Learn more" without context

### Call-to-Action Language

**Strong CTAs:**
- "See what you're missing" (discovery-focused)
- "Get your free revenue assessment" (value-first)
- "Book a consultation" (clear action)
- "Uncover hidden opportunities" (benefit-driven)

**Weak CTAs:**
- "Submit" (generic)
- "Click here" (no context)
- "Learn more" (vague)
- "Contact us" (effort-focused)

## Quality Gate Audit

### Overview

Before launching any website, landing page, or digital design, run it through this audit to ensure it feels authentically Revelate‚Äînot generic AI-generated. This checklist is the authoritative quality standard for all Revelate digital design work.

**Scoring:** 50-56 = Excellent | 45-49 = Good | 40-44 = Acceptable | 35-39 = Needs Work | <35 = Restart

---

### 1. Visual Design Audit (10 points)

**Color Usage:**
- [ ] Does the design use Revelate Navy (#1a1f3a) as the dominant color (60-70% of composition)?
- [ ] Is Revelate Cyan (#00d9ff) used strategically for highlights and insights (20-30%), not as a background flood?
- [ ] Is Magenta (#d946ef) used sparingly for critical CTAs only (5-10% maximum)?
- [ ] Are gradients ONLY used in the logo, nowhere else? (Subtle single-color gradients in navy/cyan acceptable)

**Visual Elements:**
- [ ] Does the design include the cosmic/constellation visual metaphor OR a new brand-specific pattern (not generic shapes)?
- [ ] Are shadows subtle (max 12px blur) using navy at 8% opacity, NOT heavy black shadows?
- [ ] Are left borders AVOIDED entirely? (Left borders on cards/alerts are a forbidden AI pattern)
- [ ] Are corner radiuses consistent with our system (4px-12px), not excessively rounded (24px+)?
- [ ] Is there generous but purposeful white space that creates hierarchy, not just emptiness?
- [ ] Does the design avoid glassmorphism, excessive gloss, or trendy effects?

**Visual Design Score: ___ / 10**

**Font Usage:**
- [ ] Are ALL headings using Space Grotesk (Bold 700, Semibold 600, or Medium 500)?

### Red Flags: Automatic Failure Points

If ANY of these are present, the design fails regardless of score:

‚ùå **Visual Red Flags:**
- **ANY left borders on cards/sections/alerts** (2px, 4px, or any thickness - this is a forbidden AI pattern)
- Heavy black drop shadows or excessive gloss effects
- Generic purple-to-blue gradients anywhere except logo
- Default blue (#3B82F6) or AI-tool default colors
- Generic blob shapes without brand customization
- Glassmorphism/frosted glass effects everywhere

‚ùå **Content Red Flags:**
- Buzzword overload ("revolutionary," "game-changing," "synergy")
- Vague value propositions ("Transform your business")
- Generic CTAs ("Learn more," "Click here," "Get started")
- No specific data, metrics, or proof points
- Repetitive AI-generated filler text

‚ùå **Layout Red Flags:**
- Identical three-column feature grid (icon + text √ó 3)
- Everything center-aligned with no strategic asymmetry
- Alternating left/right sections with no variation
- Footer with 6+ columns of links nobody clicks

‚ùå **Code Red Flags:**
- No semantic HTML (div soup)
- Inline styles or utility class explosion with no custom CSS
- Inconsistent spacing (random values, no system)
- Performance issues (slow load, excessive animations)
